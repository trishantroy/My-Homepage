About me

Hey, this is Trishant. I am from Kolkata, the City of Joy.

Being interested in both Electrical and Mechanical aspects of Engineering, I chose to pursue a degree in Energy Science and Engineering at one of the top universities in the country. After coming to IIT Bombay, I took part in technical activities because of my fascination with modern technology. Since then, I have been involved in technical projects on designing various mechatronic systems. I am interested in pursuing research in Controls and Motion Planning algorithms and would like to pursue a research career along this line. I am passionate about learning and developing algorithms with applications in Autonomous Robotic Systems.
