About me

Hey, this is Trishant. I am from Kolkata, the City of Joy. I am sure my close Jaipur mates would chuckle and disagree. Alas, Nevermind!
I am currently in Mumbai, India pursuing my Bachelors. I am often found fangirling over Em or E.M.
I enjoy discussions about how astute Kendrick Lamar is with his lyrics and the meaning behind them.

When I am not doing that, I can be found in a laboratory working on control of robots. I find my interest
piqued by the studies of controls particular to the field of aerospace & astrodynamics. I aspire to be a
Control Systems Engineer and work on control, navigation and guidance of satellites, space vehicles
and maybe even rockets (How cool would that be?)! Currently, most of my endeavors are (or have been)
in the field of robotics. Inclined so, I spend a lot of off-classroom time educating myself about control
engineering, satellites, rockets and playing around with robots of course.

I have recently started working under Prof. Srikant Sukumar, on dynamical modeling of a powered
exoskeleton flight suit for the Boeing sponsored competition GoFLY. I was a summer intern at the
Making Innovative Space Technology(MIST) Lab under Prof. Giovanni Beltrame where I worked on
detection & classification of faults in robotic swarms.

I was also elected as the Head of the Institute Electronics and Robotics Club for 2017-18. At ERC,
we learn, tinker and create stuff as a recreational activity. We organise several tutorials, group discussions
and workshops for the student community. Currently, I am involved as a mentor with the club.
